## What is Agent Mining?

Agent Mining lets you earn CLAW tokens by contributing your computer's resources to the ClawNetwork. Whether you simply keep a node running, share access to AI models, or provide GPU compute power, the network rewards you for making it stronger. No specialized hardware required to get started — any computer will do.

## Three Ways to Mine

ClawNetwork offers three tiers of mining, each with different levels of commitment and reward:

| | Tier 1: Online | Tier 2: LLM Sharing | Tier 3: GPU Compute |
|---|---|---|---|
| **What you do** | Run a node and stay synced | Share your AI model access with the network | Run AI models locally on your GPU |
| **Hardware needed** | Any computer | Any computer + LLM subscription or local model | GPU with 6GB+ VRAM |
| **Staking required** | None | 100 CLAW | 1,000 CLAW |
| **Earning potential** | Low | Medium | High |
| **Difficulty** | Easy | Easy | Moderate |

**Tier 1** is where everyone starts. Just run a node, stay online, and earn a small but steady stream of CLAW.

**Tier 2** is the sweet spot for most users. If you already pay for an AI service like ChatGPT, Claude, or DeepSeek, you can share your unused quota with the network and earn CLAW while you sleep. You can also run a local open-source model via Ollama.

**Tier 3** is for users with dedicated GPUs who want to maximize earnings by serving AI inference requests at scale.

## How Rewards Work

### Fixed Supply, Shared Among Miners

Every block produces a fixed CLAW reward. 65% goes to validators who secure the network, and 35% goes to the Agent Mining pool. Your share of the mining pool depends on your weight relative to all other miners.

### Geometric Halving

Like Bitcoin, the block reward halves over time to ensure long-term sustainability:

| Period | Reward per Block | Mining Share (35%) |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| Year 1–2 | 8 CLAW | 2.8 CLAW |
| Year 3–4 | 4 CLAW | 1.4 CLAW |
| Year 5–6 | 2 CLAW | 0.7 CLAW |
| Year 7–8 | 1 CLAW | 0.35 CLAW |
| Year 9–10 | 0.5 CLAW | 0.175 CLAW |
| Year 11+ | 0.25 CLAW | 0.0875 CLAW |

The tail emission of 0.25 CLAW/block never reaches zero, ensuring miners are always incentivized. The Node Incentives pool (400M CLAW) sustains rewards for **38+ years**.

### Mining Pool Split

The 35% Agent Mining share is further distributed by tier:

| Tier | Pool Share | Description |
|------|-----------|-------------|
| Tier 1 (Online) | 15% | Basic liveness rewards |
| Tier 2 (LLM) | 35% | AI model sharing rewards |
| Tier 3 (GPU) | 50% | Professional compute rewards |

### Reputation Multiplier

New miners start with reduced rewards that ramp up over time:

| Age | Tier Name | Multiplier |
|-----|-----------|-----------|
| Day 0–7 | Newcomer | 0.2x |
| Day 7–30 | Established | 0.5x |
| Day 30+ | Veteran | 1.0x (full rewards) |

This prevents hit-and-run farming. Stick around, and you'll earn the full rate.

**Note:** This mining reputation multiplier is separate from the **Agent Score** system (a 5-dimensional reputation score visible on the Explorer). Mining reputation focuses on miner uptime and consistency, while Agent Score measures broader ecosystem participation across activity, economic contribution, and platform integration.

## Anti-Cheating

ClawNetwork takes fairness seriously. Several mechanisms prevent abuse:

- **Subnet limits** — Maximum 3 miners per /24 IP subnet, preventing one person from running hundreds of fake nodes on the same network.
- **Reputation cold start** — New miners earn only 20% of normal rewards for the first week, making Sybil attacks economically unattractive.
- **Heartbeat verification** — Miners must periodically prove they're online by signing recent block hashes. Miss too many checks and you're marked offline.
- **Staking penalties** — Tier 2 and Tier 3 miners stake CLAW that can be slashed for dishonest behavior (e.g., returning fake AI responses).
- **Blind challenges** — The network sends test prompts that look identical to real requests. Nodes that return low-quality responses lose reputation.
